Why Generator Fuel Degrades — Low-Runtime Sites Are Highest Risk
  • Low fuel level → head space → condensation → free water → microbial growth
  • Bacteria & fungi metabolize sulfur → H₂S production, steel pitting, sludge
  • Polysaccharide biofilm → filter plugging, emulsification → failed starts
  • Biodiesel / diesel mix produces crude glycerin sludge at tank bottom
