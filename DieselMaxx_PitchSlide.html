DieselMaxx / GasMaxx — Inline Fuel Conditioning Device
5–15% Fuel Savings
on 39,521 Gen Sites
Passive inline device installed between tank and injectors. Steel tube with copper mesh generates electrostatic charge on fuel (flow electrification — documented in SAE literature), reducing droplet diameter, improving air/fuel mixing, and increasing combustion completeness. No magnets, no chemicals, no electronics. Applicable to any diesel genset brand.

Technology — Validated Mechanism (Under NDA)
  • Turbulent flow through copper mesh induces electrostatic charge on fuel
  • Charged fuel breaks Van der Waals bonds → smaller droplets → better atomization
  • 4 SAE technical papers independently confirm electrostatic charge improves diesel combustion
  • Authors: R. Carlson M.S. (UCLA) + E. McBride (Siemens) — independent of vendor
