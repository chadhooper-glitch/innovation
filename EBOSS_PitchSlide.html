ANA Inc. (Alliance North America) — EBOSS Hybrid Generator
72% Fuel Savings —
Live Multi-Carrier Field Data
EBOSS pairs a diesel genset with an LTO battery pack. The battery handles continuous site load; the generator only starts to recharge or cover spikes. The result: 70%+ less engine runtime, fuel, and emissions vs. a conventional running generator. Use case: deployable emergency response and pre-utility new builds — not stationary gen replacement.

Current Pain — Conventional Deployable Generators
  • Engine runs continuously at partial load — 40–60% fuel wasted on light loads
  • 500-hr oil change intervals — 6+ maintenance visits per year at remote sites
  • Cold start failures below −20°F — EBOSS Arctic pkg rated to −50°F
  • No telematics on standard gens — fuel & runtime unknown until site visit
EBOSS 70-45 — Technology Advantages
LTO Battery — 90K Cycle Life
Lithium Titanium Oxide — no thermal runaway, safe unattended outdoor use. ~20 yr life at 3 kW avg. 7-yr mfr warranty.
Hybrid Load Management
25 kWh battery handles continuous load. Gen only starts to recharge (~45 min) or cover spikes. Peak output: 70 kVA / 56 kW.
Deployable Trailer Platform
Self-contained on trailer. 80.5-gal integral fuel tank. 120/240V 1Φ & 208/480V 3Φ simultaneous output. Cam-Lok standard.
Live Telematics Dashboard
Fuel level, engine runtime, GPS, load data — visible to all carrier members in real time. ESG reporting built in.
