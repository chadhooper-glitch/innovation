Problem — Oversized WPUs Short-Cycling in Lightly Loaded Shelters

  • Dead band cycling: on at 81°F, off at 75°F — 5.1 kW compressor cooling empty shelter space
  • N+1 pair (7-day rotation) — only one WPU active, but both maintained at full readiness
