Lever 1 — HVAC Setpoint
Raise cooling setpoint within 3°F dead band (79°→82°F) during peak window. Compressor draw drops. Auto-restores after event. Battery temp protected.
Lever 2 — Rectifier Standby
Place one N+1 spare rectifier in STANDBY via HTTP POST. Removes 1.5–1.7 kW AC input. Remaining units handle full load — N+1 guarantees network safety.
Combined Effect
HVAC + rectifier = 2.5–4 kW reduction per peak event. At $10/kW/month: $300–$480/site/yr from demand charges alone.
