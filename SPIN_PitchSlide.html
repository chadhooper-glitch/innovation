Current Battery Problem — Every Site, Every Cycle
  • VRLA strings replaced every 5–7 years — thousands of truck rolls per cycle
  • NiCd requires watering, quarterly testing, hazardous disposal at end of life
  • Capacity degrades in heat — shelf life reduces exactly when outages peak
  • Battery failure = site down; SPIN flywheel has no chemical degradation
Why SPIN — Technology Advantages
Halbach Array Maglev Rotor
Near-frictionless magnetic levitation. Kinetic energy — no chemical reactions, no thermal runaway, no capacity fade.
Regenerative Self-Charging
Load fluctuations on the AC bus help maintain rotor spin speed during normal grid operation — draws less net energy.
Stackable Cube Architecture
10–15 kWh per cube. Stack cubes to meet any reserve requirement. Modular — scale up without redesigning the site.
No Maintenance Burden
No watering, no capacity testing, no replacement schedule. Operational life measured in decades, not years.
